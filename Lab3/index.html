Healthcare Facilities and Firestations in Pierce County

  • This map depicts locations of healthcare facilities and Firestations in Pierce County.

  • As shown, there are firestations are well-distributed throughout the county. However, healthcare facilities are comparably contrated in some areas, especially Tacoma. Thus, a plan for the better distribution of facilities are suggested.

  • Healthcare facilities are shown as white-colored points and firestations are shown in red-colored points. Also, Pierce County is shown in white for better understanding of the boundary.

  • Click on the points for more information and click on the menu in the left-top to turn on&off the points. Also, type in address in the "Search" tool in the right-top to find the address of your choice.

  • All data are from Pierce County GIS Portal.


Free Wifi Sites in Washington State

  • This map depicts locations of free wifi sites in Washington State.

  • As shown, there are many locations that provide free wifi service. Visit these locations when free wifi is needed.

  • Hand-held fan shaped icons are showing locations of these sites and green-colored points are added to see all the locations regardless of zoom level.

  • Click on the points for more information and also use the "Search" tool in the right-top to find any location. To see your location, click the locator button underneath the zoom buttons in the right-top corner.

  • All data are from Washington State GIS Portal.
